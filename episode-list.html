MY ROOMMATE IS A CAT

EPISODE 1:

An Encounter With The Unknown -- Subaru Mikazuki who is an antisocial, brings a cat to his home, to get inspiration in his writing novels. Kawase Akatsuki is his editor.

EPISODE 2:

I Call To You -- Buying food for the cat. Giving cat a name, Haru. The cat thinks Haru means food!

EPISODE 3:

I Touch You -- The pink ghosts. The editor likes the cat.

EPISODE 4:

For You -- Yasaka Hiroto is Subaru's best friend. The visit in the pet shop and buying collar for the cat. The first visit to vet.

EPISODE 5:

What I Want To Tell You -- Okami Nana, the pet shop worker visits Subaru's house. The diet for cat. Subaru went to Nana's house and Haru's younger brother is Nana's cat.

EPISODE 6:

What Connects Us -- Yugo Okami is Nana's younger brother. Haru's life before meeting Subaru.

EPISODE 7:

Ones Who Can't Be Controlled -- Hiroto and his siblings Hayato, Narumi, Misora, and Nagisa visits Subaru's house.

EPISODE 8:

Because You're Here -- Subaru tries to imitate Haru by sleeping on the floor and catches a fever.

EPISODE 9:

I Reach Out To You -- Subaru's book signing event.

EPISODE 10:

Eating Together -- Narumi and her friend visit Subaru's house. Subaru learns to cook for Haru.

EPISODE 11:

Feelings Crossing -- Yugo visits Subaru's house. Subaru to go on a trip for new book ideas. He visits the place where his parents wanted to go with him.

EPISODE 12:

You And I -- Subaru is stuck because of the storm and heavy rain. Haru went out of the house to look for Subaru and gets lost. Subaru is desparately searching for Haru. Hiroto, Kawase, Okami Nana and Yuga joins the search. Finally Subaru finds Haru. Everyone enjoys ramen in Subaru's house. A very happy ending of the anime.
